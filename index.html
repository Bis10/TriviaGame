Help

In order to play the game, you have to select the right option that you think is the correct answer to the question.

Then you click on the Enter Answer to submit your answer.

The correct answer will appear with a green background. If you have selected an incorrect option, it will appear in red background.

You can click on the Next Question to navigate to the next question.

On the top just below the heading are navigation buttons for questions.

If a button background is blue, it means you have not attemped the question.

If the button background is green, it means you have correctly answered the question.

If you have not answered a question correctly, the button background will be red.

The Restart button will restart the quiz if you click on it.

This popup window will be displayed when you click the Help button.
